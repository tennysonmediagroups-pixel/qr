FAQ

Questions we
hear every week

What happens at the webinar?
It's a live 30-minute session with all three coaches. We walk through the Transform system, share real client case studies, and answer questions. There's no sales pitch — just clarity on whether this is the right fit for you.
Do I need to be fit to start?
Absolutely not. We work with complete beginners through to experienced athletes. Your plan is built around where you are right now, not where we wish you were. Many of our best transformation stories started from zero.
How much time do I need each day?
The minimum effective dose is 45–60 minutes of training per day, 5 days a week. Yoga sessions are 20–30 minutes. Your coach will work around your real schedule, not an ideal one.
What if the 7-day trial isn't right for me?
Cancel within 7 days and you receive a full refund — no questions, no forms, no hassle. We're confident in the product, so we make the decision easy for you.
Can I do this from anywhere in the world?
Yes. All coaching is delivered remotely via video calls, an online portal, and messaging. You can train at home or in a gym. We have clients across 18 countries.
What makes this different from other programmes?
Most programmes sell you a plan and disappear. We sell you a coaching relationship. You get a real human being who knows your name, your history, and your goals — and who will notice if you've gone quiet for three days.
